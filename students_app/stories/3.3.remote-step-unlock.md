# Story 3.3: Manual / Remote Step Unlock

**Status:** `Draft`
**Epic:** 3. The "Manta-Signal" & Real-Time Staff Integration

### User Story

**As a** Staff Member (Manta),
**I want** to validate a student's work and unlock their next step from my dashboard,
**so that** the student can continue working immediately without me touching their keyboard.

### Acceptance Criteria

1.  **Staff Action:** On the staff dashboard (`events/[id]/appel`), if a student requires manual validation (or triggered a Manta-Signal), a "Valider l'étape" button appears next to their name.
2.  **Server Action:** Clicking this button updates the student's `steps_progress` record: advancing `unlocked_step_id`, advancing `current_step_id` (if applicable), and resetting `status` to `active`.
3.  **Camper Real-time Reaction:**
    - The student's `/(camper)/camper/[subjectId]/+page.svelte` component subscribes to its own specific `steps_progress` record via SSE.
    - When the server updates the record (staff unlocked it), the student's UI automatically transitions to the next step, accompanied by a success animation (Confetti/Green flash) **without the student needing to refresh the page**.
