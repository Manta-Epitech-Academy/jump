# Story 4.1: The Portfolio Drop-Zone

**Status:** `Draft`
**Epic:** 4. Portfolio, Valorization, and Public Showcase

### User Story

**As a** Student,
**I want** to upload screenshots of my game/code or paste GitHub/Replit links during the workshop,
**so that** I have a record of what I actually built to show others later.

### Acceptance Criteria

1.  **UI Integration:** The Cockpit (`/(camper)/camper/[subjectId]/+page.svelte`) includes a "Mon Portfolio" button that opens a side-drawer or modal.
2.  **Upload Mechanism:**
    - Students can drag-and-drop image files (PNG, JPG) or paste URLs.
    - Students can add a short caption to explain what the item is.
3.  **Database Action:** Submitting the form creates a new record in the `portfolio_items` collection, linked to the `student` and the current `event`.
4.  **Gallery View:** The drawer displays a grid of previously uploaded items for the current event, allowing the student to delete them if needed.
