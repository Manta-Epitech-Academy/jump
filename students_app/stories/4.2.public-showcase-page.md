# Story 4.2: Public Showcase Page

**Status:** `Draft`
**Epic:** 4. Portfolio, Valorization, and Public Showcase

### User Story

**As a** Student,
**I want** a public link summarizing my Epitech journey,
**so that** I can put it in my Discord bio, share it with my parents, or include it in my CV.

### Acceptance Criteria

1.  **Public Route:** A dynamic, read-only public route is created at `/p/[student-id]`.
2.  **Security:** This route does NOT require authentication. It must NOT expose sensitive PII (like email, phone number, or parent contacts). It should only display `prenom`, initial of `nom` (e.g., "Jean D."), `niveau`, and `xp`.
3.  **Content Display:**
    - The page displays the student's Total XP, Current Level (Novice/Expert), and total events attended.
    - It fetches and displays a masonry grid or list of all their `portfolio_items` (images and links).
4.  **Mobile Optimization:** The layout is highly optimized for mobile devices (as it will primarily be viewed via social media links).
